## 📋 Overview

DLOOP is **SaaS infrastructure for logistics cooperatives** in Italy. Instead of fighting Glovo on commissions, we enable cooperatives (who already have riders) to operate independently.

**The Pitch**: Provide software to cooperatives → cooperatives save €7K+/mese vs Glovo → riders become sales agents (viral growth) → breakeven in 6 months.

**Traction**:
- ✅ 4 dealers signed (€1.2K M1 revenue)
- ✅ 1 cooperativa ready (Amodio, 20 riders)
- ✅ 1 case study validated (Mario, toelettatura)
- ✅ ChatGPT WhatsApp bot live in production

**The Ask**: €50-100K seed to scale to 100 dealers + build rider dashboard + close first 5-10 cooperatives.

## ❓ FAQ for Investors

**Q: How is this different from Glovo?**
A: Glovo is marketplace (30% commission). DLOOP is SaaS for cooperatives (€149/mese + transaction fees). Zero rider employment risk.

**Q: What happens if Glovo cuts commission to 10%?**
A: Cooperatives still save because: (1) Data ownership (DLOOP customer), (2) Integration moat (18-month contracts), (3) Rider loyalty (5% commission to refer).

**Q: Who are your competitors?**
A: Deliverit (logistics SaaS), Milkman (acquired by Poste). But they focus on large retailers, not cooperatives. We're local-first.

**Q: What's your go-to-market?**
A: Amodio (signature account) → Riders become sales agents → 100 merchants in 3 months (0 CAC).

**Q: Profitability timeline?**
A: Month 6 (cumulative positive). Month 12 (monthly positive). Year 2 (30%+ net margins).
