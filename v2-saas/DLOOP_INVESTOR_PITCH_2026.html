💡Solution: SaaS for Cooperatives (Not Marketplace)

What DLOOP Does

Infrastructure for cooperative logistics (SaaS + Network Effect). We enable cooperatives to operate independently - not as "employees" but as "partners with upside".

  1. WhatsApp Bot - Order intake via messaging (zero friction for customers)
  2. Rider App (Flutter) - Real-time assignment, tracking, proof of delivery, gamification
  3. Dealer Dashboard - Analytics, customer data ownership, zero commission
  4. Rider Premium Plans - Riders become sales agents + earn more via engagement (SILVER/GOLD)
