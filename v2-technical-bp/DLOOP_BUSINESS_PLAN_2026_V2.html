5.2 Technology Stack

Component Technology Why This Choice
Backend Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions) Low-cost, serverless, real-time subscriptions built-in
Frontend (Dealer) Next.js PWA Mobile-first, offline support, progressive enhancement
Mobile (Rider) Flutter (iOS + Android) Single codebase, native performance, low bandwidth usage
AI/NLU OpenAI GPT-4 (WhatsApp integration) Natural language understanding, order parsing, customer support automation
Payments Stripe + PayPal Multi-payment methods, recurring billing for subscriptions
Infrastructure AWS Lambda (scheduled tasks), Firebase FCM (push notifications) Scalable, pay-as-you-go, minimal operational overhead

5.3 Key Partnerships

  • Payment Processors: Stripe (primary), PayPal (fallback)
  • Logistics: Self-managed rider network (no 3PL needed in Y1)
  • Marketing: Referral program (riders as growth agents), local influencers, WhatsApp marketing
  • Compliance: DPO + Legal (GDPR, Decreto Milano, food safety certifications)

5.4 Regulatory Compliance

  • Decreto Milano 2024: Rider protections (minimum wage €6/hour, insurance requirements)
  • GDPR: Data privacy, consent management, DPA with Supabase
  • Food Safety: HACCP certification support for dealer partners
  • Consumer Protection: Clear T&C, dispute resolution process

⚠️Capitolo 6: Risks & Mitigation

6.1 Market Risks

Risk Impact Mitigation
Glovo/Deliveroo Price War High Differentiation via transparency + AI, focus on dealer retention (higher margins), network effects
Slower Adoption Medium Viral loop + rider incentives (5-7% commission per dealer referral), early adopter program
Market Saturation Low (3-year horizon) Geographic expansion beyond Milan (Rome, Turin, Naples), vertical expansion (pharmacy, grocery)

6.2 Operational Risks

Risk Impact Mitigation
Rider Supply Shortage High Premium plans (SILVER/GOLD) with higher earning potential, gamification (badges, XP, leaderboards), insurance benefits
Quality/Service Issues Medium Rating system + reputation, rider SLA enforcement, customer support team (WhatsApp bot + human escalation)
Tech Outage Medium 99.9% uptime SLA (Supabase managed database), redundant payment processors, manual order handling fallback

6.3 Financial Risks

Risk Impact Mitigation
Higher CAC Medium 40% viral adoption target, referral incentives from riders, organic growth from network effects
Longer CAC Payback Medium 6-month runway sufficient to reach breakeven, Series A ready by Q4 2026
Churn Increase Low High LTV/CAC (12x), loyalty program, data ownership differentiation vs competitors

6.4 Regulatory Risks

Risk Impact Mitigation
Gig Economy Law Changes Medium Compliance-first approach (already exceed Decreto Milano minimums), legal advisors, flexible model (can employ if needed)
VAT/Tax Regulation Low Professional bookkeeping, quarterly tax filing, VAT compliance via Stripe
