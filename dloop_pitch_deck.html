The Problem: 3 Broken Systems

👥 For Customers

Pain: "I need car parts NOW"

  • ❌ 30min-2hr to get quote
  • ❌ Call 10+ shops
  • ❌ Expensive markups
  • ❌ No instant comparison

Result: 40% orders lost

📦 For Dealers

Pain: "Manual WhatsApp ordering"

  • ❌ 3-4 hours/day processing
  • ❌ 40% orders lost to delays
  • ❌ Inventory mismatches
  • ❌ Zero data/insights
