8. KEY ASSUMPTIONS & CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS
✅ Validated Assumptions:
  • Order volume 150/dealer/mese: Glovo official data = 175 avg (14% buffer applied)
  • Revenue/order €2.07: Based on verified unit economics (€0.99 service + €0.53 subscription + €0.50 variable + €0.35 delivery)
  • CAC €0: Organic rider referral model (verified with Amodio pipeline)
  • Payback 1.1 months: Unit level (independent of dealer count)
⚠️ Critical Risk Factors:
  • Dealer Acquisition Slowdown: If growth <6/month (vs target 8) → Miss break-even by 2-3 months
  • Churn >5%: If monthly dealer churn exceeds 5% → Dealer base doesn't grow
  • Amodio Signature Delay: If Amodio doesn't sign by M1 → Revenue impact -€2K/month
  • Competitor Price War: If Glovo cuts to 15% commission → Unit economics pressure
