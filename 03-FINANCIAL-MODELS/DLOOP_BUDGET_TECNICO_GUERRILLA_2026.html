💼 Modello Revenue SaaS (Subscription-Based)

NON % su venduto - Solo subscription fissa:

  • Software Fee: €149/mese per dealer (infrastruttura + supporto)
  • Transaction Fee: €0.50 per ordine processato (tech cost)
  • Setup Fee: €500 one-time per dealer (onboarding)

Vantaggi:

  • Dealer guadagna 100% su ogni ordine (noi prendiamo solo €0.50 flat, non %)
  • Ricavo predicibile (€149/mese fisso, non dipende da vendite)
  • Incentive aligned: più ordini del dealer = più fee per noi

✅ Assunzioni Critiche

Revenue Model Confidence

  • €149/mese is sustainable (Glovo costs dealers €450+/mese @ 30%)
  • €0.50/ordine covers tech infrastructure + payment processing
  • €500 setup fee is standard for SaaS onboarding

Growth Assumptions

  • 4 → 30 dealers (7.5x) is conservative vs Glovo's growth
  • 15 ordini/dealer/giorno is realistic for Napoli market
  • Organic growth from Amodio rider referrals (€0 CAC primary channel)

Team & Operations

  • CEO part-time (€800-€1.2K) while building company
  • CTO contractor (€1.5K/mese) during MVP phase
  • Support hired M2 (€500+) as volume grows
⚠️ Rischi Critici

Rider Supply

Amodio brings 20 riders. If recruitment stalls, CAC increases.

✓ Mitigation: Rider incentive program (5% commission on dealers they bring)

Dealer Acquisition

If organic growth doesn't materialize, target 30 dealers by M6 is at risk.

✓ Mitigation: Fallback to slower growth (15 dealers by M6). Still cash positive from M3.

Competitor Response

Glovo/Alfonsino could match €149/mese pricing.

✓ Mitigation: Build product moat (better app, local support, transparency). SaaS model is defensible.

Regulatory Risk

Italy labor laws might classify riders as employees.

✓ Mitigation: Legal review (€800 in CAPEX). Insurance covers liability (€600). Model them as contractors.
