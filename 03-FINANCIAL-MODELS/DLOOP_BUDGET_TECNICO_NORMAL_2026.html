⚠️ Rischi Critici & Mitigazioni
  • Rider Supply Shortage: Difficile recruire 100+ riders in 6 mesi
    ✓ Mitigation: Amodio provides 20 pre-qualified riders M1. Referral incentive €50/rider. Partner with delivery agencies.
  • Dealer Onboarding Friction: Conversion rate più bassa di previsto
    ✓ Mitigation: Pilot con 5 dealers (Toelettatura Pet, PAM, NaturaSì, Yamamay, Amodio). Collect feedback. Iterate. Simplify signup.
  • Competitor Price Wars: Glovo/Alfonsino tagliato commission rendendo 0% non defensible
    ✓ Mitigation: Build product moat (faster delivery, better app, local support in Italian). Lock dealers with 2-year contracts. Diversify revenue (data sales, analytics SaaS).
  • Regulatory Risk: Gig worker laws (riders must be employees, not contractors)
    ✓ Mitigation: Legal review ASAP (part of CAPEX €2K). Insurance €1.2K/6M. Monitor Italy labor law changes. Model employee cost scenario.
  • Team Hiring Delays: CTO/PM non disponibili quickly
    ✓ Mitigation: Pre-hire contractors before funding close. CTO already identified (Sabatino). VP Marketing from network.
  • Technology Failures: Supabase outage, Stripe integration broken, bot not responding
    ✓ Mitigation: Multi-region Supabase. Meta + Twilio fallback for WhatsApp. Stripe SLA compliance. 24/7 on-call rotation M4+.
  • Cash Burn Overruns: OPEX spikes above €9.2K/mese average
    ✓ Mitigation: Monthly budget reviews. Contingency 5% (€4.8K in OPEX). Fundraising roadmap M4 (Series A seed).
  • Product-Market Fit Uncertainty: Dealers don't see value in 0% commission (hidden switching cost)
    ✓ Mitigation: Pilot first, monetization second. Collect NPS/feedback. Test pricing (maybe 1-2% is better for sustainability).
