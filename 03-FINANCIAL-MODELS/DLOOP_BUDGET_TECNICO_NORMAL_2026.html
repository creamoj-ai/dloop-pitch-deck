💼 Modello Revenue SaaS (Subscription-Based)

NON % su venduto - Solo subscription fissa:

  • Software Fee: €149/mese per dealer (infrastruttura + supporto)
  • Transaction Fee: €0.50 per ordine processato (tech cost)
  • Setup Fee: €500 one-time per dealer (onboarding)

Vantaggi del modello SaaS:

  • Dealer guadagna 100% su ogni ordine (noi prendiamo solo €0.50 flat)
  • Ricavo predicibile (€149/mese fisso, totalmente transparent)
  • Incentive aligned: più ordini = più fee per entrambi
  • No commission war (SaaS è defensible vs % commission)

⚠️ Rischi Critici

Paid Marketing CAC Risk

Se Google Ads CAC sale sopra €500, LTV/CAC ratio deteriorates.

✓ Mitigation: Organic referral program (rider incentives). Fallback a Guerrilla model if CAC > €600.

Dealer Churn

Se retention cala sotto 95%/mese, growth rallenta.

✓ Mitigation: NPS monitoring, fast customer support, product improvements based on feedback.

Competitive Price Wars

Glovo/Alfonsino could aggressively undercut €149/mese subscription.

✓ Mitigation: Product moat (superior UX, faster support, better analytics). Lock dealers with contracts.

Team Hiring Delays

If PM/Growth hires delay beyond M2, growth stalls.

✓ Mitigation: Pre-recruit contractors before funding. Equity incentives for key hires.

Rider Supply Constraint

Model depends on rider availability for delivery execution.

✓ Mitigation: Amodio brings 20 riders M1. Rider incentive program (5% commission). Partner with delivery agencies.
